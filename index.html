About NUSA

Northeastern University Systematic Alpha (NUSA) is a premier student organization dedicated to quantitative finance and algorithmic trading. Founded by passionate students, we bridge the gap between academic theory and real-world financial markets through hands-on learning and practical application.

Our members come from diverse academic backgrounds - mathematics, computer science, economics, engineering, and finance - united by a shared interest in the intersection of technology and finance. We provide a collaborative environment where students can develop technical skills, build professional networks, and gain exposure to cutting-edge financial technologies.

Through weekly workshops, guest speakers from industry, and collaborative projects, NUSA prepares members for careers in quantitative trading, risk management, financial engineering, and fintech. We maintain partnerships with leading financial institutions and provide our members with unique opportunities for internships and full-time positions.
